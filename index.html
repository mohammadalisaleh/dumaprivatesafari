Our Story

Tanzania Is Not
Just Where We Work —
It Is Who We Are

Duma Private Safaris was founded on a single conviction: that Tanzania deserves to be experienced slowly, privately and with someone who truly knows its heartbeat.

Our founder spent years as a professional guide across the Serengeti and Ngorongoro before realising that the most meaningful safari moments happened not in large groups, but in quiet, private encounters — where time slows and Africa reveals herself fully.

Today, Duma works with a carefully curated team of expert local guides, all Tanzanian-born, all holding the highest professional qualifications — and all sharing an infectious passion for sharing their home with the world's most discerning travellers.

Where We Take You

Six Destinations.
One Extraordinary Country.

Tanzania is a land of superlatives — home to Africa's highest peak, its most iconic plains, and some of its most pristine Indian Ocean beaches. We know every corner of it intimately.

National Park
Serengeti

The world's most famous wildlife arena — endless golden plains, dramatic river crossings and the greatest show on Earth.

Best time: June – October
Known for: The Great Migration
UNESCO World Heritage Site
Conservation Area
Ngorongoro

A collapsed volcano containing an entire ecosystem — dense with Africa's Big Five within a single ancient caldera.

Best time: Year-round
Known for: Big Five density
Highest concentration of lions in Africa
Island Escape
Zanzibar

Spice-scented alleyways, powder-white beaches and Indian Ocean reefs — the perfect complement to any safari journey.

Best time: June – October
Known for: Beaches & culture
Stone Town — UNESCO Heritage Site
National Park
Tarangire

Ancient baobab trees, vast elephant herds and an untouched wilderness that rewards the traveller who seeks something beyond the obvious.

Best time: July – October
Known for: Elephant populations
Off-the-beaten-path luxury
